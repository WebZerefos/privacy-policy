4ToDay – Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 16, 2025

4ToDay (“the App”) is a personal productivity app. We respect your privacy and are committed to handling your data responsibly.

What data we collect

On‑device only: Your tasks, notes, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not operate our own servers for user data.

  • Optional iCloud sync: If you enable iCloud, Apple’s iCloud service stores your app data in your private iCloud account. Apple is the data processor for iCloud; we do not access this data.
  • No accounts: The App does not require you to create an account with us.
  • No tracking: The App does not track you across apps or websites and does not use third‑party advertising SDKs.
  • Diagnostics (optional): If you explicitly share crash logs or feedback via email, we may receive the information you choose to send.

How we use your data

We use data solely to operate the App’s features on your device. Any feedback you send is used to improve the App.

Sharing of data

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share data with third parties except:

  • Service providers you choose, such as Apple iCloud, which is governed by Apple’s terms and privacy policies.
  • When required by law, to comply with legal obligations or lawful requests.

Security

Your on‑device data is protected by your device’s security features (e.g., passcode, Face ID/Touch ID). iCloud data is protected by Apple’s security measures.

Your choices

  • You may export or delete your data from within the App where available, or by deleting the App from your device.
  • You can enable/disable iCloud sync in your device settings.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

International users

Depending on your location, you may have rights over your personal data (e.g., access, correction, deletion). Contact us to exercise these rights.
